Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find clear answers about our engineering process, pricing packages, and custom software development.

What types of custom software solutions do you build?
We build scalable digital ecosystems ranging from CRM portals and B2B SaaS platforms to Property Management Systems, custom invoicing dashboards, and interactive web tools. Everything is tailored specifically to your unique workflow, security, and operational needs.
How do you structure the pricing for websites and applications?
Our pricing ranges are based on scope and architectural requirements. Basic websites start at ₹10k - 50k, complex Web Applications average ₹15k - 2L, Native/Cross-platform mobile apps range ₹30k - 3L, and B2B Multi-tenant SaaS products start around ₹90k. We provide customized, transparent project estimations upfront.
What is your typical project development timeline?
Standard business websites are typically launched within 2 to 4 weeks. Custom web platforms, SaaS setups, or mobile applications usually take between 6 to 12 weeks, depending on design iterations, complex API integrations, and database schemas. We use active staging URLs so you can monitor progress live.
